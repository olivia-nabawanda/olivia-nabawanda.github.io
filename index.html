About Me

I am a Lecturer in Mathematics at Mbarara University of Science and Technology (MUST), Uganda. My research interests are in algebraic and enumerative combinatorics, particularly permutation statistics, generating functions, and related combinatorial structures, together with applications to other branches of mathematics.

I am also actively involved in scientific community building, student mentorship, and mathematical outreach, particularly through initiatives that support women and young researchers in STEM.

I have taught a range of undergraduate and postgraduate mathematics courses, including Linear Algebra, Abstract Algebra, Enumerative Combinatorics, Graph Theory, Real Analysis, Differential Equations, Probability and Statistics, and Calculus.
