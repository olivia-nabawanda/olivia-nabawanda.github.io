About Me

I am a Lecturer in Mathematics at Mbarara University of Science and Technology (MUST), Uganda. My research interests include algebraic combinatorics, enumerative combinatorics, and generating functions.

I am also involved in organizing scientific events, mentoring students, and mathematical outreach.
