About Me

I am a Lecturer in Mathematics at Mbarara University of Science and Technology (MUST), Uganda. My research lies in algebraic and enumerative combinatorics, with interests in permutation statistics, generating functions, and related combinatorial structures.

I am also actively involved in scientific community building, student mentorship, and mathematical outreach, particularly through initiatives that support women and young researchers in STEM.
